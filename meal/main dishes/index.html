            - Chicken fillets - 4 nos. (weighing around 100 
            gms each)
            - Salt- 1 tsp
            - Crushed Pepper- 1.5 tsp
            - Honey- 3 tsp
            - Soy sauce - 1 tsp
            - Butter - 2 dollops/ tsp
            - Olive Oil- 3 tsp (preferable but can also use 
            refined white oil)      
        
            - Lower heat to medium & keep pressing the chicken 
            pieces with the spatula for the chicken to cook & 
            get the grill marks.
            - To sauté the vegetables, take another pan & add a 
            dollop/tsp of butter. Spread it and once hot add the 
            boiled vegetables. Sauté on high for 2 mins, sprinkle 
            some salt & crushed pepper. Mix & sauté for 2 mins.
            - Serve the grilled chicken with a side of the veggies.
